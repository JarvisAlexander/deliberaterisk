About Me

DeliberateRisk.com is an attempt to memorialize my ideas and research into areas of risk measurement and risk management. Whether you think about it or not the application of risk management is an important part of everyone’s life. Like it or not, most decisions have some sort or risk analysis component to it. Whether you’re shopping for groceries, going to the gym, or working at a job, every aspect of our life entails a risk decision. I want this blog to be an exploration on the computer concepts that involve risk measurement, and risk decisions. This blog will act as a journal of the theories I wish to understand, ideas I would like to explore, and risk analysis and assessment of which I would like to master. I’m going to play around with applying the concept of deliberate risk to all areas of ones life.
