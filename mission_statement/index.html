Statement

My mission is to develop an:

Understanding the concepts of risk

Developing the appropriate tools to quantify risk exposure

Measuring the resilience to a probable risk event

Implementing methods for changing the level of risk exposure

Adjusting the degree of exposure to risk based on the level of risk tolerance

Testing and monitoring to ensure that the degree of risk exposure is not out of the desired bounds and risk assumption do not make unacceptable deviations from modeled results

Develop a vocabulary fro subjects of risk and its applicable applications * Use consistent and well defined terms

Become proficient at modeling programs * Python * Excel

Understand how to effectively use data visualization tools

Improve writing skills
